The "activities" sub-folder will contain all the activities of your project,
The "adapters" subfolder will contain the different adapters you have created for mapping your data into ListView, GridView, for example,
The "fragments" subfolder will contain the different fragments you may need to create for your project (especially in the case of a TabbedActivity),
The "interfaces" subfolder will contain your interfaces, especially when using an API,
The sub-folder "models" will contain all the models representing the entities of your application.
